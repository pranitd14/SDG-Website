17 Sustainable Development Goals

Learn about each goal and how we can achieve them together.

No Poverty

End poverty everywhere so that everyone can live a better life.

Zero Hunger

Make sure no one goes hungry and everyone has healthy food.

Good Health and Well-being

Keep everyone healthy and safe from diseases.

Quality Education

Give every child and adult the chance to go to school and learn.

Gender Equality

Treat boys and girls equally and give them the same rights.

Clean Water and Sanitation

Provide safe drinking water and toilets for everyone.

Affordable and Clean Energy

Use clean and safe energy like solar and wind power.

Decent Work and Economic Growth

Create good jobs for everyone and grow the economy.

Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure

Build better roads, technology, and factories in a safe way.

Reduced Inequalities

Make sure rich and poor people get equal opportunities.

Sustainable Cities and Communities

Make cities safe, clean, and comfortable to live in.

Responsible Consumption and Production

Use resources wisely and avoid wasting things.

Climate Action

Take action to stop climate change and protect the planet.

Life Below Water

Protect oceans, seas, and marine life.

Life on Land

Protect forests, animals, and plants on land.

Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions

Have fair laws, peace, and justice for all.

Partnerships for the Goals

Work together with all countries to achieve these goals.
