Challenges in Achieving SDGs

💰 Lack of Resources

Some countries don’t have enough money or facilities.

⚔️ Wars & Conflicts

Fights and political issues slow down development.

🌡️ Climate Change

Global warming causes floods, droughts, and disasters.

📉 Inequality

Big gap between the rich and the poor in many areas.
