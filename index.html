Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

The SDGs are 17 global goals set by the United Nations to make the world better by 2030.

What are SDGs?

The SDGs are like a plan to make the planet safer, healthier, and fairer for everyone. They cover issues like poverty, hunger, health, education, and protecting nature.
